What changed: Interview prep is no longer a scramble. The moment status moves to Interview Scheduled, the app surfaces the right stories (matched to this specific JD), the elevator pitch, and a structured checklist — all automatically. Due dates cascade from prior item completion so prep is paced, not crammed. An AI prompt guides interviewer research so the job seeker knows what to look for and how to use it. The job seeker walks in having done the work in order, not hoping they remembered what mattered.
