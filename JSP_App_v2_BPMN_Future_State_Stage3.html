What changed: Networking is no longer ad hoc or easy to skip. It is triggered automatically the moment the job status moves to Applied — no decision required. Due dates cascade from prior item completion using offsets configured in Setup, so the follow-up reminder fires based on when outreach actually went out, not an arbitrary calendar date. The AI Networking Coach drafts contextually relevant messages; the job seeker reviews and sends. Nothing goes out without the user's hand on it.
