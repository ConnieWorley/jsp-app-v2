What changed: There is no longer a scattered set of tasks living in the job seeker's head or spread across multiple tools. Every due date across every job and every stage aggregates into a single, always-current view — grouped by urgency, linked directly to the relevant job and checklist item. Completing an item automatically triggers the next due date based on the follow-up schedule configured in Setup. The job seeker opens the app and knows exactly what needs attention, right now, without hunting.
