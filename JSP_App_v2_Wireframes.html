Elevator pitches

One master pitch, with role-tailored variants for each target type. Pull the right one before a screen or interview.

Master pitch
General — AI Business Process & Operations
I am a cross-functional business analyst with a background in process design, AI integration, and operational problem-solving. I specialize in diagnosing broken workflows, redesigning them with AI-augmented solutions, and translating that work into clear documentation and stakeholder-ready deliverables. My recent focus has been on building AI-integrated tools that keep the human in the decision seat — not handing judgment over to the machine.
68 words · ~30 sec
Variant — Consulting firms
Consulting
I bring a consulting mindset to operational problems — I come in, diagnose the root cause, build the future-state design, and hand off clean documentation that the client's team can actually run. I have been applying that same rigor to AI integration, specifically around how organizations can adopt AI tools without losing process discipline.
58 words · ~25 sec
Variant — Tech / AI companies
Tech / AI
I work at the intersection of process and AI — I map how work actually flows, find where AI creates the most leverage, and build the operational infrastructure around it. I am particularly interested in teams that are moving fast on AI adoption but need someone who can slow down long enough to make sure the process underneath it is sound.
