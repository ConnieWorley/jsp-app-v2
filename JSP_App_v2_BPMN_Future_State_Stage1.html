What changed: The gut-check fit review is replaced by a structured three-lens scorecard generated automatically on save. Skills gaps are surfaced immediately rather than discovered during tailoring. Story matching happens at intake — not the night before an interview. The job seeker makes the pursue/pass decision with data, not instinct. All three AI calls run simultaneously; the job seeker reviews results, not raw JD text.
